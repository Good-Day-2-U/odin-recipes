Tacos

    If you use the traditional size hard taco shell, 
  or small 6-inch flour tortillas, 2 tablespoons of taco 
  meat per taco is the perfect amount.  This recipe, using 1 
  pound of lean ground beef, will make 12 tacos.  
  I tend to serve 3 tacos per person.  So I use 1 pound of 
  taco meat for 4 people.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Tortillas

Steps to cook

  1. Cook meat
  2. Chop veg
  3. Heat shells
  4. Make tacos
